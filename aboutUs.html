About MaRRS

MaRRS is dedicated to creating and sustaining alternative methods of education, allowing students to develop academically and personally, based on their goals, interests and abilities. Since 2003, when MaRRS first opened its doors, the company has realised that there is an ever-increasing need for education beyond the traditional school curriculum. Therefore, we have had a special focus on moving beyond the curriculum along with a commitment to help the learners achieve excellence.
Skill development and literacy are essential for a growing economy. MaRRS has been answering this call by conducting various Student Development Programmes and also providing Premium Content and Publications across the globe. MaRRS enables students to gain the knowledge they need where, when, and how they want it. MaRRS attracts thousands of diverse and dynamic students each year. Over a million students annually attend our programmes every year.
